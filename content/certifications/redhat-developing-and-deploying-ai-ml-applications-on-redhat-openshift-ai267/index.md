## Certification details

### What you will learn
- **AI/ML Application Development**: Gain hands-on experience in developing AI/ML applications on Red Hat OpenShift.
- **OpenShift AI Integration**: Learn how to integrate AI/ML workflows into the OpenShift platform effectively.
- **Exam Preparation**: Build the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the Red Hat Certified Specialist in OpenShift AI (EX267) exam.

### Why earn this certification?
- **Industry-Ready Skills**: Validate your ability to develop and deploy AI/ML applications in enterprise environments.
- **Career Advancement**: Enhance your credentials with a certification recognized by industry leaders.
- **OpenShift Expertise**: Demonstrate proficiency in leveraging OpenShift for AI/ML solutions.
